Mission Statement

Pacific Trails Resort's mission is to offer a unique experience for guests to get active and explore California's North Coast. We also strive to educate our guests about nature, the ecosystem, and wildlife conservation. We are conveniently placed next to a state park and offer guided hikes, making it a perfect activity for the entire family. We constantly strive to improve our services and amenities. Our guest's satisfaction is our top priority. Enjoy state of the art yurts with a complimentary breakfast.

Brief History

Pacific Trails was established in 1990 and has since grown to be one of San Francisco's most popular sites. We are visited daily by tourists and city dwellers who love to enjoy the breathtaking views and take in the ocean breeze. Pacific Trails has also been named the #1 Best Resort in San Francisco.

The Pacific Trails Area

Pacific Trails is located in the Golden Gate National Recreation Area, one of the most visited and largest national parks in the world. Pacific Trails was established to help make it easier for the urban population to visit national parks and experience nature's beauty. Our hiking trails start from the Golden Gate Bridge and cover over 5 miles of land.

Things to Do

  • Enjoy one of our bird watching trips with experienced and entertaining guides in the morning
  • Jump into the pool or make use of our kayaks and embark on exciting ocean adventures
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Pacific coast and the Golden Gate Bridge with our guided hikes.
  • Relax in one of our luxurious yurts, each furnished with electricity and running water.
